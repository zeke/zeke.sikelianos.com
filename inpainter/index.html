Inpainter

An AI-powered open-source magic eraser for editing images

Tue Aug 30 2022 00:00:00 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)

Inpainting is the process of using AI image generation models to erase and repaint parts of existing images.

Inpainter.app is an open-source demo that uses the Ideogram v2 and Ideogram v2 Turbo machine learning models and Replicate's API to inpaint images right in your browser.
