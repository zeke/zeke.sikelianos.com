Meta Cars of Mexico City

Futuristic vehicles seen on the streets of CDMX

Mon Jul 21 2025 00:00:00 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)

Last week at the San Francisco Book Art Fair, I happened upon a stunning publication called Meta Cars, a colorful compendium of imaginary floating muscle cars. For some foolish reason, I chose not to pop for my own copy of the mag, but this week on a trip to Mexico City I discovered that meta cars actually exist. Here are the ones I spotted around town.
