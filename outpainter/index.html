Outpainter

Paint outside the boundaries of existing images using AI

Fri Jun 23 2023 00:00:00 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)

Outpainting is the process of using AI image generation models to expand and paint outside the boundaries of existing images. Outpainting is similar to inpainting, but instead of replacing the inside of an image, you replace the outside edges.

Outpainter.app takes an image and a text prompt and then expands the canvas. The app was built in a day in collaboration with @PontusAurdal using Vue JS, Nust JS, and Tailwind CSS, and it's deployed on Vercel. ‚ö°Ô∏è
