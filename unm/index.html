University of New Mexico

Public research school in Albuquerque

Sun Sep 05 1999 00:00:00 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)

Fresh out of high school, I received a scholarship to attend the University of New Mexico in 1999. I changed my major several times, from philosophy to electrical engineering to media arts to French, and after two years of relative disenchantment, I enrolled in an English teaching program in France and made what was to be my first exodus from academia and the United States.
